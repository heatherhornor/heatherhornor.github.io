Chess America is a family run business started in 2007. Richard and Heather Hornor are a Father-Daughter team who have a passion for spreading their love of Chess! Chess America teaches chess nationwide in schools, chess camps, libraries, and park districts. Many of the students of Chess America have won or placed high in local, state and national chess tournaments. Chess America provides chess classes given by local chess professionals who share the passionate vision of Chess for all! All instructors undergo thorough background checks, and are screened for their skills as teachers in addition to their chess experience.
